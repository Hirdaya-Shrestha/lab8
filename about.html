About Me

I’m a full-stack developer from Kathmandu, Nepal, passionate about building digital experiences that feel as good as they work. With a foundation in Flutter and React, I specialize in crafting cross-platform applications with pixel-perfect UIs and robust backends.

My journey started with C++ and algorithms, evolved through Kotlin and Android, and settled into Flutter for mobile and React/Next.js for the web. I believe great software is quiet — it does its job without getting in your way.

When I’m not coding, I’m probably tinkering with electronics, reading about CPU architecture, or experimenting with new tools.
